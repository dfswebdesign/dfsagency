Regular Site Maintenance Is A Must Have!

You’ve likely already put a lot of time and effort into building your dream website (and if you haven’t, we’d love to help you with that too). You’ve spent countless hours writing the content, choosing the right imagery, determining the best user experience, and taking all of the proper steps toward marketing your brand online to help you reach your goals. But…when it comes to regular site maintenance and updates, sadly, many web design agencies fall short.

Just like your car needs to be maintained in order to run properly, so does your website. You might even want to make some improvements and upgrades along the way. It is important to understand and regularly check up on what is going on in the back-end and server side of your website. We understand that the needs of our clients vary greatly, so we have packages from 1 hour per month of website maintenance to more than 100 hours per month of ongoing web maintenance work.

All of our managed websites are monitored every 5 minutes for downtime. Why is this important? Imagine this scenario: You open up your website only to find a blank screen. How long has it been that way? Days? Weeks? With our monitoring service, we know in minutes.
